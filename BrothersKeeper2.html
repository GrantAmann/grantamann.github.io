"I am not my brother's keeper" - Cain, 3000 BC.



Brother's Keeper, Pt. 2

I do not understand it fully, but I think there that we, as human beings, are connected on a level we cannot see. This sounds cheesy and way too like canned philosophy but we are all connected. This means that my actions influence another. It also means that another person's actions influence me. I can say this, and without too many people disagreeing with me.
