GRANT AMANN

Dead Sea

July 8th, 2020





There are two bodies of water in the land that Jesus walked.

"The Sea of Galilee is teeming with fish and life,” the priest began.

“The Dead Sea is dead and doesn't support life."

"They are both fed by the sparkling water of the River Jordan. Do you know what the difference is? The Sea of Galilee gives all its water away. The Dead Sea keeps it all for itself.”

Oh, Lord, like the dead sea.
