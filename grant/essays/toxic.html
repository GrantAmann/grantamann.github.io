Toxic, Manipulative, Selfish

July 21, 2020

I am selfish. I am toxic. I am manipulative. And so are you.

Toxic, not only Britney Spears' best song, but also the 2018 word of the year. People throw this word around all the time. Especially at others. But I rarely hear people saying it themselves. How is everyone else so toxic?

This is probably pretty obvious, but I just want to write about how the definitions for toxic and manipulative are loose. I think anything can be construed as toxic, and people can label others as toxic without having to explain themselves.

I just don't think the narrative should be calling other people names.
