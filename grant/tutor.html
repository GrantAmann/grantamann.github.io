My Approach

I lived in Argentina from 2016 to 2018! During that time, I spoke and learned Spanish in a very natural way by communicating and interacting with Argentinos. My approach to teaching is similar to the way I learned myself, but easier with some tricks and hints I wish I had known! I focus on phonetics and sounds first so that accents cannot be detected, so that your Spanish ability is said without a "gringo" accent! Then, I believe it is most important to focus on verbs. This approach will actually get you able to speak and communicate with others.
